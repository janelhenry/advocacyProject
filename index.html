What is Child Labor?

Child labor is the employment of a child for labor that harms them or prevents them from attending school.

Across the globe, traffickers buy and sell innocent children. They are often transported across borders and exploited for forced labor. Due to ongoing economic hardships, these children are often coerced into harsh conditions searching for a "better" life.
